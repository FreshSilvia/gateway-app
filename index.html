WELCOME TO JAPAN!!

"Land of the Rising Sun"

Japan is an string of islands, on the eastern edge of Asia. Tokyo is the national Capital of Japan. There are four main islands: Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, and Kyushu. There are nearly 4,000 smaller islands. Japan's nearest mainland neighbors are the Siberian of Russia in the north and Korea and China farther south. Nearly four-fifths of Japan is covered with mountains. The highest peak is Mount Fuji, which is a cone-shaped volcano considered sacred by many Japanese. Even though Japan is a beautiful place, it can be dangerous. There are three tectonic plates that form the Earth's crust nearby. These three tectonic plates often move against each other, causing thousands of earthquakes to hit Japan every year. Japan also has 60 active Volcanoes.
