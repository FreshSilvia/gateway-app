Cuisine in Japan

Curry

Curry in Japan is commonly served in three main forms: curry rice, curry udon, and curry bread. It one of the most popular dishes in Japan. The most common type of curry in Japan is "Curry rice". Japan's curry has a variety of vegetables and meats including its curry sauce. One of the most popular Japan curry restaurant Chain's is "Curry House CoCo Ichibanya". Ramen

Sushi

Sushi is another famous Japanese dish outside of Japan, and one of the most popular dishes among the Japanese. In Japan, Sushi is enjoyed on special occasions usually. Sushi isnt meant to be a long,drawn out meal. The fish is meant to be eaten within 45 seconds or less, to preserve its perfect temperature. There so many Sushi chains in Japan, if you click the photo above it will take you to the top Sushi restaurant chains in Japan. Each one has many different prices which depend on your choice of sushi you like or prefer.

Tonkatsu

Tonkatsu in a very popular dish in Japan that consists of a piece of fried pork. The pork used for tonkatsu is tenderized by a mallet. Tonkatsu involes cutting the pig's back center into two-to-three-centimeter-thick slices, and then coating them with panko(bread crumps).Frying them in oil, and then serving with tonkatsu sauce,rice, and vegetable salad(mainly cabbage). The price depends on the restaurant. For more Information click the photo above.
